FAQs
What is Yegna Farm's poultry project all about?

Yegna Farm's poultry project focuses on empowering women and supporting the community by providing high-quality poultry products. We offer training, resources, and ongoing support to help women establish their own poultry businesses and contribute to the local economy.

How does Yegna Farm ensure the quality of its poultry products?

At Yegna Farm, we prioritize quality in every step of our poultry production. We maintain strict hygiene and biosecurity measures in our farms, provide proper nutrition to our poultry, and follow ethical and sustainable farming practices. This ensures that our poultry products meet the highest quality standards.

Can I visit Yegna Farm and see the poultry operations?

Yes, we welcome visitors who are interested in learning about our poultry operations and witnessing our commitment to quality firsthand. Please contact us to schedule a visit, and our team will be happy to accommodate you.

How does Yegna Farm support women in the poultry project?

Yegna Farm provides training programs specifically designed for women interested in poultry farming. We offer guidance on poultry management, business development, and marketing strategies to empower women entrepreneurs in the industry.

Can I buy poultry products directly from Yegna Farm?

Yes, we have a retail outlet where you can purchase our high-quality poultry products. Additionally, we partner with local markets and retailers to ensure wider availability in the community. Check our website or contact us for more information on where to find our products.
